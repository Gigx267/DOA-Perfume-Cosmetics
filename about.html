The DOA Journey

From a small boutique to a beloved destination for luxury fragrances and beauty essentials

Our Beginning

DOA Perfume & Cosmetics began in the heart of the Trade Fair Market, founded to bring exceptional, curated fragrances and beauty products to our community.

What started as a passion project quickly grew into a beloved destination for those seeking quality, authenticity, and personal connection in their beauty and fragrance purchases.

Today, we remain dedicated to our founding principles while continuously evolving to meet the needs of our discerning customers.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do at DOA Perfume & Cosmetics

Quality & Craftsmanship

We meticulously select each product based on its ingredients, craftsmanship, and performance, ensuring only the finest items reach our customers.

Personalized Service

Our knowledgeable team provides tailored recommendations and guidance to help each customer find products that truly resonate with their unique style.

Sustainability

We prioritize eco-friendly brands and packaging, believing that beauty should enhance both personal well-being and environmental health.
